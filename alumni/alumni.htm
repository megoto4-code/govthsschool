Objective of the Association

A. To keep liaison among the ex-students and the present students of Karimganj Government Higher Secondary School.

B. To create relationship between the ex-students and present students of the Karimganj Government Higher Secondary School for their proper guidance in the sphere of higher education.

C. To publish useful literatures, papers, magazines, books etc., without profit motive.

D. To arrange and organize lectures, debates, discussions, seminars, excursions for the diffusion of knowledge or competition in different games.

E. To promote and encourage the present students of Karimganj Government Higher Secondary School School for literary culture, scientific and technical education.

F. To help the needy students for the prosecution of studies.

G. To collect and preserve manuscripts, paintings, sculptures, works of art, mechanical and scientific instruments, books and design.

H. To engage and assists in philanthropic activities as may be deemed appropriate by the Governing Body of the society.

I. To do all such acts, deeds matters and things as may be deemed incidental or conducive to the objects.

J. To hold reunion of ex-students along with parents of present students.
